Ingredients

  • 2 cups dry navy beans
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 can tomatoes
  • 2 carrots chopped
  • 1 onion chopped
  • 1 cup chopped ham

Steps

  1. Combine beans, chicken broth, water, tomatoes, carrots and onion into a pressure cooker
  2. Cook on medium heat for 30 minutes
  3. Add in ham
  4. Cook for another 30 minutes on medium heat
  5. Salt and pepper to taste and serve
